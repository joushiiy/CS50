Tools Engineering

In my internship I was placed in the tools engineering department in a company called Turbine Overhaul Services(TOS). During my time section was upgrading and experimenting with 3D printing technology.

Tools intern

My primary job was to help prepare engineering drawings to be sent out. I occasionally will salvage different scrapped tools for reusable parts. Experiment with the 3D printer to gain more expirience with it.
